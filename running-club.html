Running Club

Build endurance, coordination, and confidence through structured running activities for ages 5 to 80.

Get Moving with Running

The Running Club provides a supportive space for participants of all ages (5 to 80) to build endurance, coordination, and confidence through structured running activities. Each three-month term introduces new techniques in pacing, breathing, posture, and safe running form. These sessions strengthen motor planning, emotional regulation, and social connection while promoting a healthy, active lifestyle.

When Select once or twice per week on a selected weekday (Monday-Friday). Available session times: 3:30-5:30 PM or 5:30-7:30 PM.
Where #204B 10190 152A St. Surrey BC V3R 1J7 - Autism Club Center
Cost 1:1 Session: $50/hour | Group Class: $40/hour
