About Us

Empowering Through AI Education

Autism Club Castlegar is a specialized educational institution in Castlegar, BC, dedicated to supporting autistic youth and adults through AI-powered learning programs.

  • Hands-on robotics and coding for logical thinking
  • AI tools training: image generation, video editing, content creation
  • Creative writing and communication skills development
  • Career preparation with digital marketing and media skills
  • Game development and interactive design workshops

Our Events

Join our vibrant community events that bring families together through celebration and competition.

Birthday Party
