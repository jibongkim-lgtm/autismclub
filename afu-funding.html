AFU Funding

Autism Club Canada is an authorized autism service provider eligible for autism funding.

How to Access Autism Funding

Authorized Service Provider

Autism Club Canada is an authorized autism service provider eligible for autism funding. Our programs are designed to meet the requirements set by the ministry, ensuring that families can access the financial support they need.

Request to Pay (RTP) Form

If you are registering with us for the first time, it is important to complete and submit the Request to Pay (RTP) Form to the ministry. This form authorizes the ministry to pay Autism Club directly for the services provided to your child.

We Are Here to Help

Please contact us for guidance on completing the form. Our team is ready to walk you through each step to ensure the process goes smoothly.

Processing Time

Note that processing may take several additional weeks, depending on the volume of submissions. We recommend submitting your form as early as possible to avoid delays in service.

Need Help with Your Application?

Contact us for personalized guidance on completing your funding forms.
